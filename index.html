About the Department

The Center for Artificial Intelligence has started in TKM College of Engineering in 2020 with a vision to explore current and future scopes and potential of Artificial Intelligence technology. The MTech branch is approved under an aided stream with an intake of 18 students. Since the program is aided and the potential of this technology is interdisciplinary, faculties of other departments handle subjects along with research supervisions.

Areas of research

  • Machine learning

    Machine learning is the study of computer algorithms that improve automatically through experience and by the use of data.

  • Deep learning

    Deep learning is part of a broader family of machine learning methods based on artificial neural networks with representation learning.

  • AI in Medical Imaging

    In radiology, AI assists in multiple processes including scheduling patients, assessing image quality, reducing radiation dose, and image interpretation.
